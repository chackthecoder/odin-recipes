Lasanga Recipe

Meat and Cheese Galore

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that combines layers of pasta sheets with rich, savory sauces and cheeses. Each layer alternates between a hearty meat or vegetable filling, creamy béchamel sauce, and tangy tomato sauce, with generous amounts of melted cheese in between. The result is a satisfying, baked casserole that's golden and bubbling on top, while soft and flavorful inside.

Perfect for family gatherings or a comforting dinner, lasagna can be customized with a variety of ingredients like ground beef, sausage, or vegetables, and often topped with mozzarella or parmesan for extra richness. Whether you're using a traditional recipe or experimenting with a new twist, lasagna is sure to please any crowd.

ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce: Cook ground beef or sausage with onions and garlic. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, and season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano. Let it simmer.
  2. Cook the Lasagna Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: Mix ricotta cheese with eggs, Parmesan, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer the Lasagna: Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then spread the ricotta mixture, followed by shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.
